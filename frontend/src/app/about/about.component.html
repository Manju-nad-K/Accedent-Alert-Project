About ResQ

ResQ is an advanced accident alert system designed to save lives during emergencies. With a single tap on the alert button, our app instantly sends your location and emergency details to your chosen contacts and local rescue teams. In critical moments, every second matters — and ResQ ensures help reaches you faster than ever before.

Whether it’s a road accident, sudden health emergency, or dangerous situation, ResQ is your trusted digital lifeline. Our system uses GPS technology, secure data transmission, and instant notifications to make sure your call for help never goes unheard.
